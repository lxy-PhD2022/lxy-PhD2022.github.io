Xinyu Li

Xinyu Li is currently a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Fudan University, and is expected to receive his Ph.D. degree in December 2026.

He received the M.S. degree in Computer Technology from Ningxia University in 2022 and the B.S. degree in Computer Science from Qingdao Institute of Technology in 2020.

His research interests lie in Embodied AI, with a particular focus on Robot General Mobile Manipulation Policy. His previous research focused on multivariate long-term time-series forecasting and 3D face reconstruction, both of which serve as important foundations for Embodied AI. He has published seven first-author papers in conferences including ACL, WWW, ICASSP, and ICME.

He is currently seeking postdoctoral positions in Embodied AI. If you are interested, please contact him by email at 22110240025@m.fudan.edu.cn.
